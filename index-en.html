The Destination

Caraíva: Where time stands still and the soul rests.

Located in southern Bahia, the Village of Caraíva is one of Brazil's most preserved and authentic destinations. Here, the streets are sand, there's no street lighting (only the starlight), and access is only by canoe, crossing the river that gives the village its name.

Walking through Caraíva is an invitation to disconnect. Nestled between the Caraíva River and the sea, you'll find a vibrant gastronomic scene, the famous forró, and frequented deserted beaches stretching as far as the eye can see. It's the perfect place for those seeking a stay in Caraíva with soul and purpose.

Whether to contemplate the sunset by the river or dive into the warm waters of Bahia, Caraíva offers a unique sensory experience where luxury is simplicity and silence is the soundtrack.

The Property

A 3,200m² sanctuary between the river and the sea.

Frequently Asked Questions

Access is from Porto Seguro (80km). By car or transfer, follow to Caraívaçu, then take a dirt road to the banks of the Caraíva River. The final crossing is by canoe (5 minutes). Cars are not allowed inside the village.
Caraíva is magical year-round. High season (New Year's, Carnival, and July) is vibrant. For true "quiet luxury" and absolute silence, we recommend March to June, or August to November.
Yes, all our bungalows have air conditioning to ensure your comfort in any season, complementing the natural ventilation of the local architecture.
We prioritize an atmosphere of silence and rest. Please consult the specific rules on each bungalow's Airbnb page before booking.
Since cars cannot enter Caraíva, you must leave your vehicle in one of the secure parking lots located on the opposite side of the river (in Caraívaçu). From there, the crossing is made by canoes that operate 24 hours.
Our breakfast is included in the daily rate and is served every day in our gourmet space. We value local products, seasonal fruits, artisanal breads, and made-to-order tapiocas.
Yes, we offer high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property. However, we encourage our guests to take the opportunity to disconnect and experience the surrounding nature.

Light clothing, flip-flops (you'll be walking on sand the whole time), sunscreen, repellent, and a small flashlight for night walks. Remember: in Caraíva, less is more.

The Journey

How to Get Here

Porto Seguro

The closest airport is Porto Seguro (BPS). From there, you can proceed by private transfer, taxi, or bus to the Caraíva ferry.

The Road

The trip takes about 2 to 3 hours, depending on dirt road conditions. We recommend 4x4 vehicles on rainy days, though common cars usually manage well.

The Crossing

Upon reaching the banks of the Caraíva River, you'll leave your car in a parking lot and cross by canoe. It's the rite of passage into paradise.

Location

In the heart of Caraíva.

We are located in a privileged area of 3,200m², with views of the Caraíva River and just 120 meters from the beach. The perfect balance between village convenience and nature's peace.
